Skyscraper
Skyscraper is a puzzle game where you place towers in such a way that the numbers on the borders of the board represent how many towers you can see in that direction.
In every row and every column, a number can only appear once.
You can use these sources of information to always deterministically place the next number.
Clicking on the title will direct you back to the homepage.
The puzzles are ascending in difficulty, so try to learn from the initial ones in order to solve the final one!.
